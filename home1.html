Frequently Asked Questions

What is ASL to Voice?

ASL to Voice is a web application that converts American Sign Language gestures into spoken words using your webcam.

How does it work?

The app interprets hand gestures and provides spoken text in real-time. It's designed for ease of use, regardless of your tech background.
